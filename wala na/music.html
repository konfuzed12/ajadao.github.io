1. "Hotel" by Kid Ink ft. Chris Brown

This song introduced me to the world of Rap/Hip-hop. I remember when I was in 5th grade, I always listened to it and sang it word for word.

3. "Bring it Back" by Twice

My favorite song from the KPOP genre. I played it on loop while playing Geoguessr because I like the beat drop in the hook. Also, my bias Dahyun wrote this song.

4. "Back to Sleep" by Chris Brown

To all the couples out there, you can listen to this song every night because it can help you be in the mood. lol. I love this song because it can cure my insomnia. It is so relaxing as Breezy is singing.

6. "Song Goes Off" by Trey Songz

I always sing this song in the shower even though I'm not a good singer. Also, I re-enact every 2000's RnB music video while singing this.

7. "Mercy" by Kanye West ft. Big Sean, 2 Chainz, Pusha T

From the greatest NBA 2K soundtrack.

8. "Drunk Texting" by Chris Brown ft. Jhene Aiko

I can relate to this song. I think everyone in this world, drunk text once in their lifetime.

9. "Monster" by EXO

My workout song because of its tempo. Also, it has a unique sound, and the rap verses were straight heat.
